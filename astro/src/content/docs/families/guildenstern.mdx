# Guildenstern

The House of Guildenstern is known throughout Western
[Betera](/locations/betera/) for their collective contribution to the
arts. Many notable members are playwrights, actors, painters, dancers and
musicians, who have contributed innovations to the crafts they practiced.

## Notable Guildensterns

| Name               | Field            | Contributions                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| ------------------ | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Karen Guildenstern | History and Lore | She was renowned for her knowledge of the histories of many ethnic groups, and was sought after by many political organizations as an asset. She eventually married into the [Rosenkrantz](/families/rosenkrantz), to Duke Bain Rosenkrantz himself.                                            |
| Paula Abdul        | Acting           | While she was a noted actor, she popularized the [Naturalist Movement of Theater](/misc/naturalist-movement). But due to questionable ethics, she was barred from using the Guildenstern name, and was subsequently added into the [Red Book of Apgar](/misc/red-book-of-apgar/) |

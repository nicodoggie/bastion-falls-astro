The *Principium* was a galley working under
[La Camaraderie de la Mer](/world/organizations/la-camaraderie/), and was its
"flagship" in that it was led by the organization's Grand Master. On paper, it
was a cargo ship that hauled iron from
[Raibon Island](/world/locations/raibon-island/) to
[Estrega](/world/locations/estrega/).

## Crew

### Captain

The Captain of the *Principium* was always the Grand Master of La Camaraderie de
la Mer. At the time of its sinking, the captain was
[Jethro Duvague](/world/characters/jethro-duvague/).

Mt Thraltor is the highest peak in the
[Ranterg Mountain Range](/locations/ranterg-mountains) and in the entirety of Senera
Island. The base of the mountain is home to the city of
[Thrantorbury](/locations/thrantorbury), which is the capital of Senera.

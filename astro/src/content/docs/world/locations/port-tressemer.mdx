*Port Tressemer* is a port town built on the mouth of the
[Samain River](/locations/samain-river) as it flows out into the
[Bay of Squids](/locations/bay-of-squids) on the northern portion of
[Raibon Island](/locations/raibon-island). Settled originally during the
[Kalassarian invasion of Senera](/locations/senera#kalassarian-invasion)

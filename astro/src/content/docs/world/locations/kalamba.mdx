Kalamba is one of the five
[Secessionist Principalities](/world/organizations/secessionist-principalities/)
that broke away from [Kolha](/world/locations/kolha) during the
[Revolt of the Kolhan Ansa](/world/events/revolt-of-the-kolhan-ansa/) (1250-1254
AI). It borders Kolha to the west.

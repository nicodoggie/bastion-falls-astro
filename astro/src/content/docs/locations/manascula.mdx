# Manascula

Manascula is a protectorate of the [Ahurian Empire](/locations/ahuria). It was
annexed in 1235 by a joint effort between the Purha and the Chetsi. It sits at
the westermost edge of the [Tarsan](/locations/tarsa) continent.

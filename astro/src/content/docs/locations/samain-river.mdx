The main river on [Raibon Island](/locations/raibon-island). It bisects the island
almost equally in a north-south direction. It sources its water primarily from
groundwater collected in a
*[freshwater lens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lens_\(hydrology\))*, the
[Dustain River](/locations/dustain-river), as well as snow-melt from the
[Montrabar Mountain Range](/locations/montrabar-mountain-range).

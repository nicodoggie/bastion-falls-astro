*Kurris Wudward* is a 52-year-old personal servant of
[Henry](/characters/henry-surrata-spellvig/) of the Spellvig branch of the House Surrata. Sold
as a slave to the Spellvigs as a young man, he was later freed by Sonia
Surrata-Spellvig prior to the Seneran Great Slave Emancipation in 1235 AI.

## Early Life

Kurris was born in of Senera. As a young man, he was sold as a slave to the
Spellvigs of the House Surrata.

Andrei Delarde was a founding member of the infamous mercenary group
[Free Theater](/organizations/free-theater/) along with its leader,
[Paula Abdul](/characters/paula-abdul/). He was once a fellow at the
venerable
[Apgarian Magical Military Academy](/organizations/apgarian-magical-military-academy/),
before he was dismissed due to charges of embezzlement of public funds.

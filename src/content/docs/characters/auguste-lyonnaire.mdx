Auguste Lyonnaire, aka *Le Petit Tigre*, is the captain of the cog
[Souris Marine](@/vehicles/souris-marine). During the
[Poasan-Apgarian Conflict](@/events/poasan-apgarian-conflict), he was the
captain of a pirate ship that regularly brought supplies from the
[Parpelissian Coast](../locations/parpelisse.mdx) through the Seneran blockade,
into [Sanery](../locations/sanery.mdx) to augment the resistance forces within.

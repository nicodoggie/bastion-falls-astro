The McFarland family has roots in the northern island kingdom of
[Iutland](../../locations/iutland.mdx). A branch of the family had migrated
southwards, and settled in [Raibon Island](../../locations/raibon-island.mdx).

Some of them have intermarried with the [Savoy](../savoy/index.mdx) of
[Port Rainoso](../../locations/port-rainoso.mdx). The branch of the family is
well-known for being competent lawyers or accountants.

Mir is one of the five
[Secessionist Principalities](../organizations/secessionist-principalities.mdx)
that broke away from [Kolha](./kolha.mdx) during the
[Revolt of the Kolhan Ansa](../events/revolt-of-the-kolhan-ansa.mdx) (1250-1254
AI). The principality takes its name from the historical
[Mirian Peninsula](./mirian-peninsula.mdx).

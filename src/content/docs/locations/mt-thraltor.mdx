Mt Thraltor is the highest peak in the
[Ranterg Mountain Range](./ranterg-mountain.mdx) and in the entirety of Senera
Island. The base of the mountain is home to the city of
[Thantorbury](./thantorbury.mdx), which is the capital of Senera.

# Manascula

Manascula is a protectorate of the [Ahurian Empire](./ahuria.mdx). It was
annexed in 1235 by a joint effort between the Purha and the Chetsi. It sits at
the westermost edge of the [Tarsan](./tarsa.mdx) continent.

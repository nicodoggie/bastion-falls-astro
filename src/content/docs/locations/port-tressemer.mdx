_Port Tressemer_ is a port town built on the mouth of the
[Samain River](./samain-river.mdx) as it flows out into the
[Bay of Squids](./bay-of-squids.mdx) on the northern portion of
[Raibon Island](./raibon-island.mdx). Settled originally during the
[Kalassarian invasion of Senera](./senera.mdx#kalassarian-invasion)

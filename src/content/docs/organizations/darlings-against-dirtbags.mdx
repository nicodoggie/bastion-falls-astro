Darlings Against Dirtbags was an organization that taught Nuran women of various
ranks, ages and creeds to defend themselves with the martial art _Capoeira_. The
art was pioneered by Arzhadadi peasants who needed to be able to practice
without being caught by their Kalassarian oppressors
